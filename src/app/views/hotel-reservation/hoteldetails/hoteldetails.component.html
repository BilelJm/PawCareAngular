A pet hotel is a facility that offers temporary accommodation and care for pets, typically cats and dogs, while their owners are away. These hotels provide a range of services such as feeding, exercising, grooming, and medical care.

The pets are usually housed in individual kennels or suites, with some hotels offering more luxurious options like private rooms or spa treatments. The goal of a pet hotel is to provide a safe and comfortable environment for pets while giving their owners peace of mind knowing their pets are well taken care of.
